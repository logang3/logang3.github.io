Fitness/Healthy Eating Challenge

Work out every day, for 30 days straight.
Avoid carbs, sugars, and processed foods.

Details

Workouts: even if its only 15 or 20 minutes, get a workout in. Its important to not overdue, so still only aim for 3–5 serious strength workouts per week.
Food: the goal is to eat as much natural fruit, veggies, and meats as possible. Cutting out processed foods while maintaining calorie intake will force you to eat a lot more veggies and fruit.
